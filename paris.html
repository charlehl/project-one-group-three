Data Plots of Paris:

These are the plots for Paris that we used for our project analysis. As you can see in the first figure the passenger travel very closely follows the average temperature. You can see that the passenger travel even dips a bit when the average temperature maxes out in July. There are also small peaks in travel during December to January time frame which would mostly likely be due to holiday travel for Christmas/New Years.
