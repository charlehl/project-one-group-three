Linear Regression Analysis Comparison

These two linear regressions above show the two general types of cities that we found. One where there is a clear pattern of passenger travel increasing as temperature increases(Paris) and one where it did not and seemed more random (Cancun). The anomaly we found in the latter was able to be explained by plotting average rainfall by month. In the below images you can see that where the temperature rises and the passenger travel falls shows a larger rainfall average in that corresponding month. For the example given, Cancun's hurricane season coincides with the dip in passenger travel.
